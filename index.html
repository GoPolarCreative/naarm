Wurundjeri Country, Built Into Every Job

Naarm Earthmoving is a proud Aboriginal-owned and operated business of the Wurundjeri Woi Wurrung people. With 7 years in the earthmoving industry and 15 years of experience in Aboriginal cultural heritage, we bring an understanding of Country that no other operator can.

When you hire Naarm, you're not just getting a quality earthmoving team. You're supporting Indigenous enterprise and genuine connection to the land we work on — from residential builds to civil infrastructure.

We operate with the values of mob — honesty, hard work, and standing by what you do.
